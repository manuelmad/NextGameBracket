How to Use NextGameBracket

Game List

  • Enter a game title and press the “Add to list” button (you can also press Enter key). Repeat this process for all titles you wish to add.
  • Each title on the list can be edited, by clicking on its name, or deleted, by clicking the corresponding “x”.

Grand Arena

  • Select the bracket type based on the number of titles participating (4, 8, or 16).
  • Drag any title from the Game List (or Battle Stage) to any bracket slot. Here, you can replace any title by dragging another onto its slot, or delete it by clicking the corresponding "x". You can also drag a title from one slot to another empty slot to copy it.
  • Match the titles you want to see in the polls so the winners advance through the bracket. You have to put each winner in the next round's slots manually.
  • Finally, drag the champion title onto the crown slot.

Battle Stage

  • Drag any two games from the Game List (or Grand Arena) to their respective VS. slots to pit them against each other.
  • Here, you can replace any title by dragging another onto its slot, or delete it by clicking the corresponding “x”.
  • Set the poll duration in the web app. Start the poll in your YouTube live stream chat so your audience can begin participating.
  • Immediately, press the “Start” button in the web app to begin the countdown (you can “Pause” and “Restart” the countdown, if needed) so your audience can see how much time is left to vote.
  • When the timer reaches 0, check the poll results in your YouTube live stream chat and announce the winner. Winner advances to the next round in the bracket.

NOTE: This web app automatically saves your progress so you can resume from where you left off if your browser closes or refreshes.
